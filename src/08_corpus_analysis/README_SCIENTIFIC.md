# Stage 07: Corpus Analysis - Scientific Documentation

## Research Objectives

- Generate comprehensive corpus statistics
- Analyze corpus characteristics and distributions
- Compare corpus properties across datasets

## Research Questions

1. What are the key characteristics of the corpus?
2. How do corpus properties vary across different datasets?
3. What statistical patterns exist in the corpus?

## Hypotheses

- **H1**: Corpus statistics reveal key dataset characteristics
- **H2**: Distribution analysis identifies important patterns
- **H3**: Cross-corpus comparisons highlight dataset differences

## Dataset

- **Input**: Final processed datasets from all pipeline stages
- **Output**: Corpus statistics and analysis reports
- **Metrics**: Size, composition, distributions, comparisons

## Methodology

- **Descriptive Statistics**: Mean, median, quartiles, distributions
- **Distribution Analysis**: Publication years, genres, ratings, review counts
- **Cross-Corpus Comparison**: Statistical tests comparing datasets
- **Visualization**: Plots and charts for corpus characteristics
